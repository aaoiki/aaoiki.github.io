A brief story

TL;DR Before CS college, I had a great passion for programming.

I was first introduced to programming at the age of 11 by our neighbor who was a software developer. He gave me a version of Visual Basic 5 on a weird sticky thing called a flash drive (yup, they were new). I installed VB and started looking up the internet for VB scripts and how to run them. I dropped my jaw when I could eject the CD-ROM drawer using VB code! It was some kind of a fun game and it was all about passion.

After high school, it was very obvious to me that I should join a programming-related field, and I did, I joined a CS faculty in one of the reputable universities in the country.

I do respect the staff. They are great people. I don’t want to put this as a way to hate or to deny
