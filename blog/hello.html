Here’s a new beginning with a new blog.

I should write more

This is one of the things I want to do this year so I will try to keep up with it. Part of why I didn’t write so often is being afraid to be judged. I wrote many blog posts and deleted them on my previous blog just because of that. It is such a great issue that affects a lot of what I’m doing and I’m trying to overcome that |_・). It even affected my code somehow. So I hope writing more will help me with this issue.

Why a new blog?

As I mentioned above, I had another blog (I just took it down) where I barely wrote anything for more than a year. I though it might be a good motivation for me to write more if I started in a new blog. I made some changes to the theme for better readability. I’m probably getting a new custome domain for this blog soon.

What I will write about

I will write many of my thoughts here. Mainly about coding and my journey as a young programmer. Also I will write in whatever language it fits (yes, it’s a multilingual blog). I think that words appeal in languages (their own language) more than others. I might try to translate between two or more languages as a hobby.
